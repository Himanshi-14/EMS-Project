About EMS

Event Management System (EMS) is a web-based platform designed to manage events efficiently. It allows students to view upcoming events, register online, and stay updated with campus activities.

The system is developed as part of a research-based implementation to provide an easy and structured event management interface.

Developed by: Himanshi Jambhulkar
